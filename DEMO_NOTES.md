# Demo Notes

## Suggested Opening

Hi, this is TaktLedger, a workflow automation prototype for converting handwritten machine shop production documents into structured, validated, reviewable operational records.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the TaktLedger dashboard.
2. Show dashboard metrics, shift summary, machine summary, and validation issue summary.
3. Go to the Upload page.
4. Upload one sample machine shop image.
5. Show the uploaded document preview.
6. Click Upload & Process Document.
7. Show the extracted records table.
8. Point out high, medium, and low confidence badges.
9. Point out validation warnings/errors.
10. Edit one incorrect or uncertain field.
11. Re-run validation.
12. Save reviewed records.
13. Go to History and show the uploaded document.
14. Open the previous upload.
15. Go to Records and show search/filter behavior.
16. Go back to Dashboard and show analytics updated.
17. Mention `README.md`, `AI_WORKFLOW.md`, and assumptions/tradeoffs.

## Suggested Closing

The main focus was not just OCR, but a complete human-in-the-loop workflow from document upload to extraction, validation, review, history, and analytics.
